Cleanup Today — Camera Roll Cleanup App

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the app work? Cleanup Today shows you photos and videos from today's date in previous years. Review them, keep what you want, and delete the rest.
  • Will my photos be permanently deleted? No. Deleted photos go to your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album, where they remain for 30 days before being permanently removed. You can recover them at any time within that window.
  • Does the app upload my photos anywhere? No. Everything stays on your device. The app never accesses the internet or sends your photos anywhere.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchase." Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.
