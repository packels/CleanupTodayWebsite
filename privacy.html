Privacy Policy

Cleanup Today — Effective March 15, 2026

Overview

Cleanup Today is a camera roll cleanup app that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fundamental to how the app is designed.

Data We Do Not Collect

Cleanup Today does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no server-side components. We have no way to access your data because it never leaves your device.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library using Apple's PhotoKit framework. This access is used solely to display your photos and videos within the app and to delete items you explicitly select. Photos and videos are never copied, uploaded, or shared. All processing happens locally on your device.

Notifications

The app can send local notifications to remind you to clean up your camera roll. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. No notification data is sent to any server.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Cleanup Today does not process, store, or have access to any payment information. Purchase records are managed by your Apple ID.

On-Device Storage

The app stores your progress (completed days, streaks, and statistics) locally on your device using UserDefaults and Keychain. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is used only to display your progress within the app and its Home Screen widget.

Third-Party Services

Cleanup Today does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporters, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at cleanuptoday@packels.com.
